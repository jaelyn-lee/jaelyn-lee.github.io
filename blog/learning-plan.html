Learning Plan

What is your long-term goal or career pathway?

It sounds very easy, but it is not. Because I want to be financially, mentally and physically stable to support my family. I want to live a satisfied and happy life even if it's just me and my dog, or me and my partner. I still have a lot of things I want to achieve and have a long way to go, so life as a family seems to be too far away for me, but someday I want to be a person who makes a happy family and delivers the love and appreciation I received from others.

From a career perspective, I want to become a software engineer or a full-stack developer who is recognised by co-workers and satisfied with my job. And I want to spend time with my family and friends in Korea while working remotely and have quality ME time!

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My strength is that I have a goal-oriented, responsible, and problem-solving mindset.
As I grew up in Korea, I developed a result-oriented and goal-oriented tendency. Some people might say that this is not a good thing for collaboration, but I want to achieve and prove what I'm doing, so I always work responsibly and participate in collaboration as best I can. And if there's any problem, I try to find a way to solve them first instead of looking for something to be responsible for.

My biggest limitation is lack of concentration.
I can easily lose concentration because I can't focus on one thing for a long time. However, concentration is essential to work and study, so I set a certain amount of time to focus and rest for my brain. For example, I've been using 25 minutes to focus on my work without being disturbed by anything, and 5 minutes to take a break and thankfully it works!

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I hope the human skill develops through this course. I didn't realise until I started this course that I have never asked myself much about who I am, what I like to be, and what I want, and this process will be a meaningful time to reflect on what I really want to be and what I want to achieve.
