My identity and Values

My personality

I'm an Ambivert (a blance of Extrovert and Introvert) and I'm very shy in front of strangers, but if you're open and ready to be my friend, I'm so ready for you! I'm usually very calm and quiet, but when I get close, I become a very cheerful, bubbly, and weird person. You shouldn't think of me as a quiet person because I usually hide my personality!
While my family is fiery and quick-tempered, I have a calm and gentle personality in my family. However, since I am Korean, I can't help but endure the frustration and try to do it quickly.

My Strengths & weaknesses

Strengths

I am a outcome-driven and goal-oriented person, so if I start, I have to see the end. As I get older, I don't challenge as recklessly before, but I still like to challenge, and I think and decide a lot before I start, but I try to finish well once I start. I'm also a very good team player. As a team member and team leader, I always think for the team and lead the team to success with responsibility. Since I am patient and have good eyes for details, I think I will be able to solve it until the end without giving up easily even if there are difficulties or obstacles in this course.

weaknesses

In a new environment, I might be reluctant to let myself out in a way that might help me make new friends or join a new community. But if I get used to it and there are kind people around me, I open up easily.
Also, I lose concentration easily, and I often get caught up in other thoughts easily. To solve this problem, I am overcoming this limitation by using the Pomodoro study method to block all things that may interfere with my studies and focus on studying for a fixed time.
