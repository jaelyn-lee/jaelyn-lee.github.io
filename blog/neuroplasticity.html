Research Growth Mindset

what it a Growth mindset and why it is relevant?

A growth mindset simply means that people believe their intelligence and talents can be improved and grown through effort and actions. People who have the growth mindset like challenging and know the Power of yet. And now you know you have a chance to get smarter than you are right now.

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

I already knew that mindset is impacting a person’s thoughts and actions but I learnt how I talk to myself can change my mindset from the fixed to the growth mindset. I often use negative words to myself to whip me to focus hard and achieve something but the articles explaining the growth mindset demonstrated that giving a positive and no limits on yourself is helping you bounce back from ‘failure’ by increasing effort. I realised that actions that self-absorbed and negative thoughts of me eventually made my chances even less, and I thought I should trust and follow my process with more positive words to me.

How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

  • Reward the process, not the outcome - I will think it will be okay to be slower than others and trust the process and reward the process, not the outcome for myself to not think I am a loser and failed because I am learning things slowly.
  • Get out of my comfort zone - I will stop avoiding difficulty and will challenge myself until I fix the problems and find the solutions.
  • Accept failure as part of the process - failure, setbacks, and initial confusion are all part of the learning process. Fake it until you make it.
